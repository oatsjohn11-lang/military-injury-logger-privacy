Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2025

Introduction

Military Injury Logger ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Our app allows you to store the following information locally on your device:

  • Injury descriptions and medical information
  • Body part selections and injury locations
  • VA claim documentation and status
  • Medical treatment history
  • Photos and documents you choose to attach
  • Dates, severity ratings, and other injury-related data

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Upload your data to any cloud servers
  • Transmit your information over the internet
  • Share your data with any third parties
  • Access your data remotely

Your information is encrypted and protected by your device's biometric security (Face ID/Fingerprint) and remains under your complete control.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • Local device storage only
  • No internet connectivity required

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Export: You can export your data at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual entries or all data
  • Control: All data remains on your device under your control

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. If you uninstall the app, all local data is automatically removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not collect any analytics or usage data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Google Play Store policies
  • Apple App Store guidelines
  • HIPAA principles for medical data (local storage only)
